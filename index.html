About

Ana Paula Martínez is a Mexican photographer from Mexico City, based in Madrid since 2023. She works across architecture, interiors, and reportage, moving between digital and analog formats.

She has been taking photographs since she was 18.Currently in her second year of graphic design, she’s also drawn to fashion and architecture, influences that subtly inform her visual approach.

Her work leans toward capturing the essence of spaces and moments, with an emphasis on light, composition, and atmosphere. With a close attention to detail, her images explore how environments hold emotion and identity.

For her, photography is a way of giving shape to the chaos of everyday life. She is interested in growing through collaboration and creating visual stories that speak quietly but with intention.
